BUILD-P2 - Async Social World

Goal:
Prove that one player can help another player asynchronously and receive visible social feedback.

Success criteria:
- player A contributes to a shared campus project;
- player B can benefit from that contribution;
- player A receives a visible social signal;
- the app has a feed or social surface showing that the contribution lives in the world;
- repeated requests and reload do not create duplicate rewards.

Do NOT:
- add Exam;
- add complex seasons;
- add guilds;
- add PvP;
- add new archetypes beyond the base three.

Focus:
Not the number of mechanics, but the clear feeling that "my contribution helped another player."
