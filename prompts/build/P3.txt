BUILD-P3 - Cooperative Exam

Goal:
Create a small cooperative event that requires a party and where role composition meaningfully affects the outcome.

Success criteria:
- player can create or assemble a party;
- party can enter the Exam event;
- participant roles affect probability and quality of result;
- successful and partially unsuccessful outcomes differ;
- event result is written into the social space of the game.

Do NOT:
- add PvP;
- add deep season meta;
- add battle pass;
- add marketplace;
- add unrelated independent modes.

Focus:
Cooperation, readable role dependency, and weekly culmination.
